SB-20 — Hanoï

The hanoï tower game. Transfer tower from column 1 to column 2 or column 3 in the least number of moves (7 moves is least for a 3-disk tower). Program will trap illegal moves, such as transfer of a larger disk on top of a smaller one, or attempted transfer from an empty column.

  • Initialize:  RST   CLR   R/S 
  • Enter a move in the for from.to (e.g: 1.2 move from column 1 to column 2):  R/S
