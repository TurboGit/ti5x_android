SY-23 — Triangle Solution (1)

Knowing certain combinations of the attributes (sides or angles) of a triangle, the unknown attributes and the area can be calculated.

Knowing SSS

First, enter the sides of the triangle, in any order:

  • Enter side a: a  A 
  • Enter side b: b  B 
  • Enter side c: c  C 

Then, compute angle A:  2nd  A´

Now, you can compute the remaining angles, in either order:

  • angle B:  D 
  • angle C:  E 

Knowing SSA

First, enter the known sides and the angle, in any order:

  • Enter side a: a  A 
  • Enter side b: b  B 
  • Enter angle A: A  C 

Then, compute side c:  2nd   B' .

Now, you can obtain the remaining angles, in either order:

  • angle B:  D 
  • angle C:  E 

Knowing SAS

First, enter the known sides and the angle, in any order:

  • Enter side a: a  A 
  • Enter side b: b  B 
  • Enter angle C: C  C 

Then, compute side c:  2nd   C' .

Now, you can obtain the remaining angles, in either order:

  • angle B:  D 
  • angle A:  E 

To calculate Area
  • Select program 24,  2nd   Pgm   24 
  • Calculate Area, press  2nd   C' 

Formulas Used

A + B + C = 180° or 2π

c2 = a2 + b2 - 2ab cos C

a ÷ sin A = b ÷ sin B = c ÷ sin C

Registers used: 01-06.
